Focalize

Attention Creatives!

Focalize is your one stop to track all your current and past project.

Get Organized!

After you sign up all you need to do is click the create button on your dashboard. Then give it a name and describe what your project is, who it is for, set a target date for completion, and any fees charged to your client if applicable.

Update as you go!

Update your progress as you move towards the due date by adding notes on accomplishments and milestones or you can use it to add any additional information.

Focalize is for everyone!

The platform is “project agnostic” meaning no matter what you are working on you can create a project for it. Whether you are a freelancer or just a hobbyist. A painter or a coder. After you finish, just mark it complete to remove it from your pending project list and into a completed project list where it will be available to review.
